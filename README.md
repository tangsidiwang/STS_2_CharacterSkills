# STS_2_CharacterSkills
Slay the Spire 2 StS2
Two skills were added for each character, and two independent actions were added for each character, including skills and ultimate moves.

Cooldowns carry over between battles, with ultimate moves starting on cooldown. Attacks and kills charge the ultimate.
为每个角色添加了俩个技能，给每个角色添加了俩个独立操作，技能和绝招。

冷却跨战斗，开局时绝招处于冷却中，攻击和击杀都会给大招充能

# skill-sts-2 Character Skill Summary

Last Updated: 2026-03-16

Ironclad:
Skill: Empower - Gain 2 Temporary Strength.
- Cooldown: 3 turns
- Max Charges: 1
Ultimate: Terminator - Choose an enemy. Deal 22 damage and Stun it. Lose 5 HP.
- Cooldown: 70 turns
- Cooldown Recovery: 0.3 per attack / 1 turn per kill

Silent:
Skill: Flip - Draw 1 card.
- Cooldown: 7 turns
- Max Charges: 2
Ultimate: Create - Consume all Energy. Obtain 3 random Potions.
- Cooldown: 60 turns
- Cooldown Recovery: 0.3 per attack / 1 turn per kill

Defect:
Skill: Source - Gain 1 Temporary Focus.
- Cooldown: 7 turns
- Max Charges: 3
Ultimate: Startup - Trigger all Orb passive effects once, then deal 10 damage to all enemies.
- Cooldown: 50 turns
- Cooldown Recovery: 0.4 per attack / 1 turn per kill

Heir:
Skill: Star Strike - Deal damage equal to the number of Radiant Stars.
- Cooldown: 6 turns
- Max Charges: 1
Ultimate: King's Judgment - Forge 5, then gain Block equal to your Monarch Sword damage.
- Cooldown: 50 turns
- Cooldown Recovery: 0.3 per attack / 1 turn per kill

Calamity Mage:
Skill: Touch of Calamity - Apply 3 Calamity to all enemies, then instantly kill enemies whose Calamity exceeds their current HP.
- Cooldown: 5 turns
- Max Charges: 2
Ultimate: Undead Binder - Summon 8, and all enemies lose 8 Strength for 1 turn.
- Cooldown: 50 turns
- Cooldown Recovery: 0.5 per attack / 1 turn per kill
